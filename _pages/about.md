<p>PhD candidate at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering University of Ioannina, working on query processing and optimization for Business Intelligence environments under Prof. Panos Vassilidis. My research focuses on shifting the querying and answering paradigm of traditional OLAP operations, using an abstraction layer that captures the intented analytics tasks of the analyst and not the detailed actions of the analysis and presenting the analysis results that are of most significance for the analyst. My recent efforts are focusing on the design and optimization of intentional operators for the automatic extraction of interesting hidden data properties (Highlights) from multi-dimensional datasets.</p>

<p> I am a graduate of the Computer Science and Engineering department of University of Ioannina, having completed a 5-year integrated Diploma (EQF Level 7). I am part of the <a href="https://www.cs.uoi.gr/~pvassil/projects/datastory/"> DataStory: Intentional database querying to facilitate interactive data storytelling </a> project, that is implemented in the framework of <a href="https://www.elidek.gr/">H.F.R.I </a> call “3rd Call for H.F.R.I.’s Research Projects to Support Faculty Members & Researchers” (H.F.R.I. Project Number: 23640).</p>
